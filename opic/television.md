Watching these programs is the best way for me to relax and escape from work stress after a long day."

2.2. When do you watch TV most often? Where do you watch it? Whom do you watch with? Plus, what do you do while watching TV?
Bối cảnh dễ nhớ: Xem vào buổi tối (in the evening), tại phòng khách (living room), xem một mình (alone) hoặc với gia đình, vừa xem vừa ăn nhẹ (snack).

Script siêu đơn giản đạt AL:

"I usually watch TV in the evening after finishing all my work. 
I prefer watching it in my living room because there is a big screen and a comfortable sofa.

Most of the time, I watch TV alone so I can completely focus on my favorite shows. 
However, sometimes I watch with my family on weekends. 
While watching, I also like to enjoy some snacks or drink a cup of tea. It makes the experience much more comfortable."

2.3. What were TV programs like when you were young? How are they different now? Tell me about how TV programs have changed over the years.
Bối cảnh dễ nhớ: Ngày xưa TV ít kênh, đen trắng/chất lượng thấp (few channels, low quality). Ngày nay có Smart TV, nhiều kênh, chất lượng HD (Smart TV, many channels, high quality).

Script siêu đơn giản đạt AL:

"When I was young, TV programs were very limited. 
There were only a few channels, and the picture quality was not very good. 
We had to wait for a specific time to watch our favorite cartoons.

But now, things have changed completely. 
With Smart TVs and the internet, we can watch thousands of programs anytime we want. 
The quality is amazing, with beautiful colors and clear sound. 
I think TV programs today are much more interesting and convenient than in the past."

2.4. How did you first become interested in watching TV? What kinds of programs did you like watching when you were a child? How has your interest in TV programs changed over the years?
Bối cảnh dễ nhớ: Hồi nhỏ thích xem phim hoạt hình (cartoons) với anh chị em. Lớn lên chuyển sang xem tin tức, thế giới động vật (news, animal world).

Script siêu đơn giản đạt AL:

"I remember I first became interested in TV when my parents bought our first color television. 
When I was a child, I was big fan of cartoons like Tom and Jerry. 
I used to watch them every afternoon with my sibling, and we laughed a lot.

Over the years, my interest has changed. 
Now that I am an adult, I rarely watch cartoons. 
Instead, I prefer watching global news, business, and tech shows to stay updated with the world. 
I think my taste became more practical as I grew up."
