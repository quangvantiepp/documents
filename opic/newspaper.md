Để đạt được cấp độ **Advanced Low (AL)** trong bài thi nói (như OPIc hay các bài thi đánh giá năng lực nói tương tự), bạn không cần phải dùng từ vựng quá phức tạp hay câu quá dài. Bí quyết là **phát âm rõ ràng, ngữ điệu tự nhiên, phản xạ tốt và cấu trúc câu rõ ràng** (có mở, thân, kết).
Dưới đây là các câu trả lời mẫu (scripts) cho chủ đề **Newspapers (Báo chí)** được thiết kế với từ vựng ngắn gọn, dễ nhớ, tự nhiên nhưng vẫn đủ ý để đạt mức điểm mong muốn.
### Bộ 1: Chủ đề Báo chí & Thói quen đọc
#### 1-1. Tell me about one of your favorite newspapers and why you like it.
 * **Ý chính:** Kể tên tờ báo yêu thích và lý do (tin tức nhanh, thiết kế dễ đọc).
 * **Script mẫu:**
   > My favorite newspaper is *Vnexpress News*.
   I like it because it is very easy to read. 
   It is very popular and the design is clean, so I can find the news quickly. 
   It covers many different topics, like sports, technology, entertainment and lifestyle. 
   I can always find something interesting to read on my way to work.
   > 
#### 1-2. Tell me about your routine when you read a newspaper. Where do you like to read the newspaper? And how do you choose what to read first?
 * **Ý chính:** Thói quen đọc ở đâu và chọn tin tức như thế nào.
 * **Script mẫu:**
   > I usually read the news on my smartphone during my morning commute.
   When I open the app, I always look at the main headline first to see what is happening in the world. 
   After that, I scroll down to the technology section because I want to know about the technology changes. 
   If a title catches my eye, I will click and read the full story.
   > 
#### 1-3. How did you first become interested in reading newspapers? What attracted you to reading newspapers when you were younger?
 * **Ý chính:** Bắt đầu từ khi nào và tại sao lại thích lúc nhỏ.
 * **Script mẫu:**
   > I started reading newspapers when I was in high school. 
   At that time, my father bought a physical newspaper every morning, and I used to look at the sports and entertainment pages. 
   I was attracted to the colorful pictures and short, funny stories. 
   Over the years, this habit grew, and now I read the news every day to stay updated.
   > 
### Bộ 2: Các loại báo & Tin tức đáng nhớ
#### 2-1. What kinds of newspapers are there in your country? What kind of news do they typically report on, and what are some sections they have?
 * **Ý chính:** Các loại báo ở Việt Nam và các chuyên mục chính.
 * **Script mẫu:**
   > In my country, there are both printed newspapers and online news websites. 
   They usually report on politics, business, and social events. 
   Most newspapers have a few main sections: the front page for breaking news, the business section for the economy, and the lifestyle section for health and entertainment. 
   Personally, I prefer the online versions because they update news much faster.
   > 
#### 2-2. Talk about a recent news story you read or saw. What was it about? Who was involved?
 * **Ý chính:** Kể về một tin tức gần đây.
 * **Script mẫu:**
   > A recent news story that caught my attention was about a new high-speed train project in my country. 
   The government and foreign companies are working together to build it. 
   It is very important because it will save a lot of travel time between major cities. 
   Many people are excited about this new development.
   > 
#### 2-3. We all have news that we are particularly interested in. What was a story you read or saw that interested you? What made it more memorable than other news stories? What were your thoughts after you encountered that story?
 * **Ý chính:** Câu chuyện đặc biệt, lý do nhớ và suy nghĩ.
 * **Script mẫu:**
   > A story that interested me was about a local volunteer group that helped clean up a dirty river in my city. 
   It was memorable because the group had no budget, but they still made a big difference. After reading it, 
   I realized that small actions can really change our environment. 
   It inspired me to think about how I can contribute to my own community.
   > 
### Bộ 3: Sự thay đổi của báo chí
#### 3-1. How are newspapers different today than how they were in the past? What has changed with regard to how the news is reported today?
 * **Ý chính:** Khác biệt giữa quá khứ và hiện tại.
 * **Script mẫu:**
   > In the past, people read printed newspapers and had to wait until the next morning to get updates. 
   Today, everything is digital. 
   We can get breaking news instantly on our phones. 
   Also, news is now reported with videos and interactive pictures, making it much more engaging than before.
   > 
#### 3-2. Newspapers are going through a time of transition from paper to digital formats. Talk about this change to digital forms of reporting of the news. How has it affected the way people get their news?
 * **Ý chính:** Tác động của việc chuyển đổi sang báo điện tử.
 * **Script mẫu:**
   > The change from paper to digital formats has made a huge impact. 
   People do not buy physical papers anymore; instead, they read the news through social media and mobile apps. 
   It is very convenient because we can check the news anywhere and anytime. 
   However, it also means we have to be careful about fake news on the internet.
   > 
### Bộ 4: Phỏng vấn phóng viên
#### 4-1. Imagine that you are interviewing a reporter for a local newspaper in order to ask about his/her job. Ask the reporter three or four questions to find out more about what they do.
 * **Ý chính:** Đặt câu hỏi phỏng vấn phóng viên (3-4 câu).
 * **Script mẫu:**
   > "Hi, thank you for your time today.
   I would like to ask you a few questions about your job. 
   First, what do you like most about being a reporter? Second, how do you find new story ideas? And finally, 
   what is the most challenging part of your work?"
   > 
#### 4-2. During the interview, you get an emergency call from your office and you need to stop the interview and get back to work quickly. Explain the situation to the reporter and make arrangements for the rest of the interview at a later time.
 * **Ý chính:** Xin lỗi ngắt lời vì việc gấp và hẹn lịch tiếp theo.
 * **Script mẫu:**
   > "I am so sorry, but I just received an urgent call from my office and I need to go back right now. 
   Can we continue this interview at another time? Maybe this Friday afternoon? 
   I will call you to confirm. 
   Thank you for understanding!"
   > 
#### 4-3. Tell me about one memorable experience you had related to reading newspapers. It might have been a news story you read or an interesting editorial. Tell me about the experience in detail.
 * **Ý chính:** Trải nghiệm đọc báo đáng nhớ.
 * **Script mẫu:**
   > A memorable experience happened last year when 
   I read a story about a young student who overcame many difficulties to win a scholarship. 
   It was so moving that 
   I shared it with my friends and colleagues at work. 
   The story reminded me never to give up on my dreams, no matter how hard things get.
   >
